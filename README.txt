It's a XO game,where you play against computer.
There is a matrix 3x3.
You choose to be X or O.
You have to enter your sign in matrix,until someone win,or all fields are filled.
You pull your move with entering the index values of free position on the table.
Index values for the first row are:0 0 , 0 1 ,0 2
For the second row:1 0 , 1 1 , 1 2
For the third row:2 0 , 2 1 , 2 2 
Try to beat program in this game!
